## Measuring Lead Generation ROI<p class="text-[#86868B] mb-6 leading-relaxed">You cannot improve what you do not measure.</p><p class="text-[#86868B] mb-6 leading-relaxed">Yet most contractors have no idea which lead sources actually produce profitable jobs.
