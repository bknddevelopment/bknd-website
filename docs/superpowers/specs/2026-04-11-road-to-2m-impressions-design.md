## 2. The Math

2,000,000 impressions / 30 days = ~66,667 impressions/day.

**How GSC impressions work:**
- Position 1-10 (Page 1): ~100% of search volume = impressions
- Position 11-20 (Page 2): ~10-15% of search volume
- Position 21+: <5%, diminishing rapidly

**Implication:** We need either massive page-1 coverage (hard with no authority) or enormous long-tail breadth where many low-KD keywords are achievable in 30 days.

**Each well-written article generates impressions for 50-200+ keyword variations** — not just the target keyword. This multiplier is our main lever.

## 3. Core Strategy: 3-Prong Attack

| Prong | What | Why |

| Content Blitz | 170 editorial articles across 5 content types | Volume is the #1 lever. Each article captures 50-200 keyword variations. |
| Programmatic SEO | 200 templated location/service pages | Thousands of long-tail queries with minimal per-page effort |

## 5. Impression Acceleration Tactics

Beyond content volume, these tactics compress the timeline:

### 5a. Google Discover Optimization (5-10 trending articles)

Articles designed for Discover feed can generate 50,000-200,000+ impressions in a single day.

**Requirements:**
- Large, high-quality hero images (1200x675px minimum)
- Trending/newsworthy topics in AI and tech
- Published within hours of news breaking
- Strong E-E-A-T signals (author bio, byline)

**Example topics:**
- "[Latest AI tool] launched — what it means for small business"
- "Google just changed [X] — here's what marketers need to know"
- AI industry news with BKND's take

### 5b. Aggressive Indexing Pipeline

- **IndexNow API integration** — Ping Bing/Yandex/others instantly on publish
- **GSC URL Inspection API** — Request indexing for every new URL
- **XML sitemap** — Auto-generated, updated on every publish, submitted to GSC
- **Internal links from existing indexed pages** — Every new page linked from at least 2 existing pages to help crawlers discover it

### 5c. Social Signal Amplification

- Every article shared on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, relevant Reddit subreddits
- Drives initial traffic which sends engagement signals to Google
- Reddit posts in r/webdev, r/smallbusiness, r/SEO, r/marketing drive both traffic and backlinks

### 5d. Internal Linking Architecture

- **Hub pages** for each pillar (e.g., /comparisons, /pricing-guides, /locations)
- Every article links to 3-5 related articles within and across pillars
- Breadcrumb navigation with schema markup
- "Related Articles" component at bottom of every post
- Goal: no page more than 3 clicks from homepage

## 6. Technical Implementation Requirements

### 6a. Content System Scaling

Current system: `src/lib/blog.ts` (monolithic ~8K line file) + `src/lib/posts/` (individual post files)

**Required changes:**
- Migrate to scalable content directory structure:
  ```
  src/content/
  ├── comparisons/       # 50 articles
  ├── cost-guides/       # 30 articles  
  ├── best-of/           # 40 articles
  ├── industry-guides/   # 20 articles
  ├── alternatives/      # 30 articles
  └── locations/         # 200 pages (generated from data)
  ```
- Location page generator reading from a cities data file
- Dynamic route generation for each content type
- Shared components: ComparisonTable, PricingTable, FeatureGrid, FAQ, AuthorBio

### 6b. SEO Infrastructure

- **Dynamic XML sitemap** — generated at build time from all content
- **JSON-LD schema per content type:**
  - Articles: Article + FAQ schema
  - Comparisons: Article + FAQ + ItemList schema
  - Location pages: LocalBusiness + Service schema
  - Cost guides: Article + FAQ + HowTo schema
- **Meta tags:** Unique title, description, og:image for every page
- **Canonical URLs** on all pages
- **Robots.txt** with sitemap reference
- **IndexNow** endpoint for instant index requests

### 6c. Performance Requirements

- Maintain static export (fast TTFB)
- Lazy load images below fold
- Keep bundle size under control despite 370+ pages
- Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1

## 9. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Slow indexing | Medium | High | IndexNow, GSC API, social amplification, internal links from indexed pages |
| Thin content penalty on location pages | Medium | High | Ensure genuinely unique content per city, not just name swaps |
| Keyword cannibalization across articles | Low | Medium | Clear keyword mapping, canonical URLs, distinct intent per article |
| Content quality issues at scale | Medium | Medium | Established templates, editorial review process, AI-assisted but human-edited |
| Google Helpful Content Update flags | Low | High | Focus on genuine value, expert perspective, original data where possible |
| 2M in 30 days not achievable | High | Low | The content infrastructure pays off at day 60-90 regardless |
